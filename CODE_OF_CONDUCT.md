# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and productive
environment for everyone who contributes to StoryBridge AI, regardless of
background or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in discussions and reviews.
- Offer and accept constructive feedback in good faith.
- Assume good intent and ask for clarification before escalating.
- Credit the work of others and collaborate openly.
- Keep conversations focused on the project and its goals.
- Help newcomers get started.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment of any kind.
- Discriminatory language or imagery targeting any group or individual.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, issues, or pull requests.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Any other conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate
  in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Concerns about conduct can be reported to the project maintainer by opening
a confidential GitHub issue or emailing `conduct@example.invalid`. Reports
will be reviewed promptly, kept as private as practical, and may result in
warnings, temporary restrictions, or removal from the project at the
maintainer's discretion.
